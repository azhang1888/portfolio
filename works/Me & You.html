GOALS
  • Create emotionally compelling narrative and gameplay within a 3-day timeline.
  • Navigate complicated romantic relationship through an unreliable narrator and game mechanics.
  • Understand the game development process, from ideation to production to completion, better.
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Wrote emotionally-compelling branching dialogue and choices, with 2 alternate endings, for greater player immersion.
  • Collaborated with two other writers to create cohesive gameplay and story across different writing styles.
  • Proposed, playtested, and rescoped “swords” mechanic to allow the team to finish within deadline.
  • Maintained documentation for a 4-person team.
  • Edited scripts to balance clarity and mystery for an unreliable narrator.

WHAT I LEARNED
  • 1. Documentation. Even though the team was relatively small, that didn’t mean we were immune to confusion. Keeping clear documentation of each change, with agreed upon narrative and gameplay goals, meant everyone on the team was up to date with the state of the game. This became especially vital with our deadline rapidly approaching; we didn’t have the time to brief everyone if we didn’t read the documentation. Also, just having text to reference if you were tired or unsure was life-saving.
