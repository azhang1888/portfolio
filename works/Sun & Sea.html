GOALS
  • Create a narrative-driven game, exploring an alternative of the Icarus myth.
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Outlined Sea Route and wrote branching dialogue for 2 separate endings, according to player choices.
  • Scripted dialogue into Twine, while communicating gameplay goals and expectations with a 3-person team.
  • Focused on creating an immersive mythological Greek environment through prose and Homerian style for players.
