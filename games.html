Player Profiles
During my time as a paid Game Master (GM), I encountered a wide variety of players. It got me thinking—what were the general trends among their playstyles? More importantly, how could I create better (and more fun!) worlds for them to engage with? To improve my storytelling skills and craft more impactful narratives for my players, I began surveying them about safety tools and documenting what I’ve observed.
